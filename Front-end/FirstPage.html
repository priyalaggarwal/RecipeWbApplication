How to Use

Follow the steps below and cook your favorite recipe with us.

  • SEARCH

    Search recipes by category in our category selection box. Search recipes by name.

  • SELECT

    Do you have a limited number of ingredients in your fridge and are too lazy to go to the supermarket? Just grab those ingredients from the fridge and from our list of ingredients and click next.

  • DONE!

    Get the recipes from your choice of ingredients. Pick one and start cooking with Kitchen Ninja!
